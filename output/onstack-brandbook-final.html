01 — Brand Story
What is ØnStack
ØnStack is the regulated infrastructure layer that connects traditional foreign exchange with stablecoin settlement. We are not an exchange. We are not a wallet. We are the rails.

Replace manual, opaque cross-border banking layers with programmable, compliant FX technology — starting with Brazil.

Become Latin America's primary regulated hub for cross-border capital movement — bridging traditional FX and stablecoin infrastructure.

05 — Tone of Voice
Precise. Not corporate.
We write like an engineer who respects your time. Specific over abstract. Numbers over adjectives. One sentence over three.
Do

"BRL → USDC. Settled on Morph L2. BACEN reported. 3.2 min average."

Don't

"Experience lightning-fast cross-border payments with our revolutionary platform!"

Do

"Your transfer is settled. IOF: R$ 190. IRRF: exempt. Tracking: BCB-2026-0847291."

Don't

"Congratulations! Your money is on its way! 🎉"

Do

"73% lower cost than SWIFT on a US$100K transfer. R$ 984 vs R$ 3,655."

Don't

"Save big on international transfers with our amazing low fees!"

Do

"SPSAV-authorized. BACEN Res. 519/520/521 compliant. FX license active."

Don't

"We're fully licensed and regulated for your peace of mind."

VOICE PRINCIPLES
Precise
Numbers, not adjectives. "3.2 min settlement" not "fast settlement". Every claim is verifiable.
Direct
Lead with the answer. No preamble, no filler. Respect the reader's time and intelligence.
Trustworthy
Cite the source. Name the regulation. Show the math. Transparency is our competitive advantage.
