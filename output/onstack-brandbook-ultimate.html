Brand Story
ØnStack is regulated infrastructure. Not an exchange. Not a wallet. The rails that connect traditional foreign exchange with stablecoin settlement — across Brazil and ADGM.
Mission
Eliminate the structural cost and delay of cross-border payments for Brazilian businesses, exchanges, and iGaming operators through regulated stablecoin infrastructure — at a fraction of SWIFT's cost and in a fraction of the time.
Vision
To be the dominant FX infrastructure layer for Brazil–global corridors — the single API that exchanges, fintechs, and enterprises trust when the corridor is complex, the compliance risk is real, and the settlement window cannot wait.
Position
We are not another FX broker. We are the only player in Brazil with both SPSAV authorization and an active FX license — operational from day one via strategic acquisition, not a 24-month regulatory roadmap.

Do — Consistent Size

Use 16px for inline text icons, 20px for list items, 24px for standalone features, 32px for empty states. Never mix sizes within a component.

Tone of Voice
ØnStack speaks with the clarity of an expert who has done the work — not the confidence of one who claims to have. Primary archetype: The Sage. Secondary: The Ruler.
PRECISE
Every claim is quantified. Every number is sourced. We say "<5 minutes," not "fast." We say "73% cheaper," not "affordable." Shadow: avoid pedantry — be exact, not exhaustive.
DIRECT
State the point in sentence one. Active voice. Short sentences. No preamble. "Your SWIFT transfer took 4 days. We settled it in 4 minutes." Shadow: blunt without context reads as dismissive.
TRUSTWORTHY
Trust is not claimed — it is constructed. We name partners, cite regulations, show the math. "SPSAV-compliant from day one — not a roadmap, a fact." Shadow: over-disclosure undermines confidence.

Writing Principles
Numbers Not Adjectives
Quantify every benefit claim. If you cannot put a number on it, the claim is probably not strong enough to use. "Fast" means nothing. "4m 12s" means everything.
Active Not Passive
"We settle in <5 minutes" not "Settlements are completed in under 5 minutes." Subject → verb → result. Every time. Passive voice signals institutional hedging.
Specific Not Vague
Name the partner: "Bybit," not "a leading exchange." Name the regulation: "BACEN Res. 519/520/521," not "applicable regulations." Vagueness signals uncertainty.

Photo Direction
Do — Photo Style

Real people at work: CFOs reviewing dashboards, treasury managers on calls, developers at terminals. Muted, warm color grading. Natural light. Brazil and UAE contexts.

Don't — Photo Style

No stock photo handshakes. No fake smiling office scenes. No rocket ships or "futuristic" imagery. No coins or generic "crypto" imagery that signals speculation.
