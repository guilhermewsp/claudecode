Mission

Replace manual, opaque cross-border banking layers with programmable, compliant FX technology — starting with Brazil.

Vision

Become Latin America's primary regulated hub for cross-border capital movement — bridging traditional FX and stablecoin infrastructure.

Tone of Voice
We write like an engineer who respects your time. Specific over abstract. Numbers over adjectives.
Do

"BRL → USDC. Settled on Morph L2. BACEN reported. 3.2 min average."

Don't

"Experience lightning-fast cross-border payments with our revolutionary platform!"

Do

"73% lower cost. R$ 984 vs R$ 3,655 on US$100K."

Don't

"Save big on fees with our amazing low-cost solution!"

Do

"SPSAV-authorized. BACEN Res. 519/520/521 compliant."

Don't

"Fully licensed and regulated for your peace of mind."

09
Illustration & Photography
Minimal geometric line art. Cool-toned photography. No crypto clichés.
Do — Photography

Urban infrastructure, financial districts, clean workspaces, São Paulo skyline, Abu Dhabi ADGM. Cool-toned, desaturated, high contrast.

Don't — Photography

Handshakes, generic stock people smiling, rockets, gold coins, "to the moon" imagery, neon cyberpunk aesthetic.

Illustration style
Geometric circles + horizontal lines. Sparse. Gold accent at 15-40% opacity.
